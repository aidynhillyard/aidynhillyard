    Recent Projects

    GROW

    In GROW, a text-based RPG programmed with React, you have recently survived an accident that resulted in permanent paralysis and dissociative amnesia. You move back to your childhood town, but you don't remember the town, or the people in it. You have the opportunity to grow plants and trade them with townspeople as you try to understand more about who you were... and who you could be.

    Restroom Resource (API)

    By pulling data from the Refuge Restroom API, The Restroom Resource allows users to search by keyword for all-gender restrooms in a variety of countries. Users can then add restrooms to their list to view additional details and save them to browse later. Restroom details include location, directions/instructions, and whether the restroom is accessible and/or has a changing table.

    GoalGetter (MERN)

    GoalGetter allows a user to brainstorm project ideas and put them into motion. The user can add, edit, delete, and explore both groups and projects in order to turn their dreams into manageable goals.
